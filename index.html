About NRS Solar Solution

NRS solar solution is a leading provider of solar rooftop and solar trading services. With a mission to promote sustainable and renewable energy sources, we are dedicated to offering professional solar panel installation, maintenance, and repair services to businesses, individuals, and organizations.

Our vision is to establish a world-class solar farm and solar panel installation, maintenance, and repair company that is not only recognized in one state but also in other cities across the India. Our aim to make photovoltaic panels the primary source of energy for india, reducing the reliance on nonrenewable energy sources and minimizing the impact on the environment.

NRS Solar Solution has made significant strides in the solar industry in recent years with integrated solar production, solar pumps, street lights ,ACDB,DCDB with the aim of introducing a new, more cost-effective and reliable solution for the entire solar energy industry.
